\section{Expected Results and Figures}
\label{sec:results}

The current repository can generate trajectory, control, safety, coverage, and
planner logs. However, the existing Gazebo videos should be treated as
qualitative visualization until the offline planner reaches a verified
coverage threshold on the target set.

The paper should include the following figures:
\begin{itemize}
  \item sampled ISS inspection targets colored by inspected/uninspected state;
  \item planned three-dimensional trajectory in LVLH coordinates;
  \item executed trajectory compared with planned trajectory;
  \item coverage ratio versus time;
  \item cumulative $\Delta v$ versus time;
  \item minimum clearance versus time;
  \item method comparison table over Monte Carlo runs.
\end{itemize}

The qualitative Gazebo figure should show the NASA ISS mesh and the chaser
following the planned trajectory, but the quantitative claim should come from
the logged coverage and safety metrics.
